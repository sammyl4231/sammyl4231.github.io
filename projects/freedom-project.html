Sammy's Freedom Project

My freedom project is called Crocodile Chemistry!

I made this project choosing the topic of Chemistry at the beginning of my sophomore year and part of the Software Engineering program. I had many challenges and takeaways from doing this project. My challenges were resizing and repositioning the bootstrap card and carousel, plus the color of the text. My takeaways were that I learned to resize and repostition the card and carousel using `margin: auto;` in the `style.css`. Then when I finished and resolved my issues, it was time to present my freedom project to the class. Then after presenting a slideshow to the class, it was then time to present my actual freedom project to a whole bunch of people. I felt embarassed going up in front of a whole bunch of people. But then at the end I got used to it, and didn't feel shy to share my project with so much more people.
